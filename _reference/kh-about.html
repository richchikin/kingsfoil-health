About Kingsfoil Health

We believe healthcare deserves better

Kingsfoil Health is a modern health insurance brokerage built to restore dignity to the way businesses and their people experience healthcare.

Why we started

The health insurance industry is built around convenience for carriers and brokers, not for the businesses buying coverage. Most companies get shown the same fully funded plans year after year because that's the path of least resistance for everyone except the client.

We saw companies paying more every year with no visibility into why, no control over outcomes, and no one in their corner advocating for a better approach.

Kingsfoil Health was built to change that. We bring modern plan structures, proactive claims management, and a concierge employee experience to businesses that deserve better than the status quo. We're based in Utah, serving the companies and communities we're part of.

[ Photography: team working together, warm natural light ]
Our mission

Restore dignity to American healthcare through compassion and modernization

We can't fix the healthcare system from the top down. But we can help businesses and their people navigate it smarter, get a better experience, and build something more financially sustainable. One company at a time.

Our vision

Become the most trusted brand in the health ecosystem

Through enduring advocacy for care recipients, solving one problem at a time. Trust isn't claimed. It's earned through consistently showing up with clarity, transparency, and care.

We serve with H.E.A.R.T.

Five values that change how we actually operate, not just what we put on a wall.

H
Honor
We disclose our commissions upfront. Every dollar, every incentive, in writing, before you sign anything. The Consolidated Appropriations Act requires it. We'd do it anyway.
E
Excellence
We use concierge tools, not call centers. Your employees get real human guidance navigating care. Not a phone tree. Not a chatbot. A person who knows their name.
A
Accountability
We share your claims data with you. Year-over-year performance tracking, transparent reporting, and honest assessments of what's working and what isn't. No black boxes.
R
Respect
We treat your benefits budget like our own money. Every recommendation starts with what's right for your company and your people. We'll tell you if staying on your current plan is the best option.
T
Trust
We're a year-round partner, not a once-a-year salesperson. Proactive check-ins, claims monitoring, and renewal strategy that starts months before your renewal date. You'll never wonder if we're paying attention.
